MENU

Appetizers Samosa - KES 450 Crispy pastry filled with spiced meat or vegetables. Bhajia - KES 500 Sliced potatoes coated in spiced chickpea flour batter and deep-fried. Kachumbari - KES 350 Fresh tomato and onion salad with a hint of chili and lime. Main Courses Nyama Choma - KES 1,500 Grilled marinated beef or goat served with kachumbari and ugali or fries. Chicken Pilau - KES 1,200 Spiced rice dish with tender chicken pieces and aromatic spices. Tilapia Fry - KES 1,400 Whole fried tilapia served with ugali or rice and a side of sukuma wiki. Matoke - KES 1,000 Stewed green bananas with beef or chicken in a flavorful tomato sauce. Githeri - KES 900 A hearty mix of boiled maize and beans, seasoned and sometimes mixed with vegetables. Vegetarian Dishes Sukuma Wiki - KES 800 Stir-fried collard greens with onions and tomatoes, served with ugali. Irio - KES 850 Mashed potatoes mixed with peas and corn, served with a side of vegetables. Vegetable Curry - KES 900 Mixed vegetables cooked in a rich, spiced coconut curry sauce, served with rice or chapati. Sides Ugali - KES 200 Traditional maize flour porridge, a staple in Kenyan cuisine. Chapati - KES 250 Soft, layered flatbread, perfect for scooping up curries and stews. Mukimo - KES 350 Mashed potatoes with greens and corn. Maharagwe - KES 400 Spiced kidney beans cooked in coconut milk. Desserts Mandazi - KES 350 Sweet fried dough similar to a donut, often enjoyed with tea. Mango Pudding - KES 450 Creamy mango dessert with a hint of cardamom. Kaimati - KES 300 Sweet, syrup-coated dough balls, crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside. Beverages Dawa - KES 300 Kenyan cocktail made with honey, lemon, ginger, and hot water. Chai - KES 250 Traditional Kenyan tea brewed with spices and milk. Passion Fruit Juice - KES 350 Freshly squeezed passion fruit juice. Tusker Beer - KES 500 Popular Kenyan beer. Soda - KES 200 Assorted soft drinks.
